Exciting Expeditions

We are passionate river guides dedicated to unforgettable white water experiences. Safety, stewardship, and pure fun on the river since 1999.

History

Our company started with a single raft and a dream: make the river accessible to everyone. Over the years, we have grown into a seasoned guide team with trips for all skill levels.

Today, we operate on multiple rivers and invest in conservation, local communities, and guide training. Your adventure supports healthy waterways for future generations.
